My biggest hobbies are tech-centric. I'm a software/web developer, first and foremost. (In an alternate universe, I'd probably have become a graphic designer.) Currently, I help maintain the University of Delaware Computer & Information Sciences department's Discord server, including its custom-made bot, Sage, I'm an undergrad contributor for human-computer interaction-related research at college, and I'm an intern software developer who works with JavaScript and React.

I've been interested in computers for as long as I can remember, having played video games a lot as a kid. I started programming with Java in 2015, and I've learnt a lot since. I'm proficient in C#, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Java, TypeScript, and Python. I also know a bit of C and Node.js and am currently learning C++, Assembly, and React.

I have a bit of 3D modelling experience using Blender, but it's not something I actively pursue. I also have worked with the Unity game engine, though my forte in that regard is scripting.
