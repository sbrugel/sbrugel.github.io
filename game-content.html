South London to Brighton 2009 Scenario Pack

Release: 19 August 2020

This scenario pack contains 9 scenarios for the South London to Brighton route, available on Steam Workshop. All scenarios are based in 2009, a complete different scene from the Brighton Mainline today, using the actual timetables and diagrams from the year. As per reality, the actual formations of certain AI services may slightly deviate from the diagrams laid out. Each drive is set in different conditions, from clear winter days to stormy summer evenings.

Transport for Wales 2020 Scenario Pack

Release: 1 May 2020

This download contains 5 scenarios for the South Wales Coastal/Welsh Marches Merge route, set in the present day, giving you over 12 hours of driving time in the Class 150, 158, and 175. There is additionally 1 bonus scenario for North Wales Coastal: Crewe to Holyhead.

Wherry Lines Scenario Pack

Release: 1 May 2020

This download contains 9 scenarios covering 11 services for the Wherry Lines route, set in various years. Trains covered include the Class 37, 68, 156, and 170. The total driving time across all scenarios is 7 hours.

Railways of Devon & Cornwall 1980s Scenario Pack

Release: 4 April 2020

This download contains 10 scenarios covering 12 services for the Railways of Devon & Cornwall route, set in 1980. Trains covered include the Class 31, 43, 50, and 117/118. The total driving time across all scenarios is 12 hours. An additional two scenarios are included for the DPS Barnstaple to Exeter route, albeit continuations of scenarios for RDC. All scenarios use the 1980 timetable. Loco allocations based on depot and coach formations have been made as accurate as possible, given the resources I have.

Cornish Mainline Scenario Pack

Release: 13 January 2020

This download contains 2 HST services (covered in a total of 9 scenarios), operated under the GW franchise, which travel to Penzance. The three routes used are Western Mainlines, Railways of Devon & Cornwall, and South Western Expressways to Reading. The scenarios are set in various time periods in varying conditions. All scenarios are set to the timetable of the relevant year, and all liveries/formations are also correct to those of the year set. A few things are added to spice up your experience such as adverse signals and a TSR.

ECML South - WAGN/FCC Scenario Pack

Release: 11 January 2020

A project started in late 2019, this download contains 6 scenarios for the DPSimulation East Coast Mainline South route and the Backdated Train Sim Moorgate Extension, all set from 2004-2014, a heavy transitional era for the ECML, the London to Peterborough / Cambridge section in particular. All scenarios are set to the timetable of the relevant year, and all liveries/formations are also correct to those of the year set.

Saturday Morning in Wales

Release: 23 November 2019

Starting at Chester DMUD, prepare 175115 + 011 for service and take the service ECS to Bangor to form a service to Maesteg, dividing at Llandudno Junction to form the Llandudno shuttle. Due to engineering works at Holyhead, all services are truncated to Bangor today. Then, take 175115 on the first leg of 1V93, 07:07 to Maesteg, as far as Chester. Finally, you'll resume the service at Newport and take it to Bridgend.

Wherry Lines Short Set Scenario Pack

Release: 18 July 2019

This is a pack of two scenarios for AP’s Wherry Lines. This gives you a total of 75 minutes worth of driving in the Class 37 top-and-tailed “short set,” set in the present day and using real timetables and formations, as well as the correct locomotive numbers on both runs.
